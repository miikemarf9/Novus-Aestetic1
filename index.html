Anti-Wrinkle Injections

A subtle, refreshed appearance — softening fine lines and wrinkles with precise, medical-grade injectable treatments.

From £150

Anti-wrinkle injections gently relax the muscles responsible for expression lines across the forehead, frown and around the eyes. The result is a smoother, well-rested look that still moves and feels like you — never frozen.

Every treatment begins with a full consultation and facial assessment to make sure it's suitable and tailored to your features. Results typically develop over two weeks and last around three to four months.

Premium Dermal Fillers

Natural volume, definition, and structural balance — tailored to complement your unique features.

From £220

Dermal fillers restore lost volume and enhance natural structure across the lips, cheeks, chin, jawline and more. Using premium, medical-grade products, treatment is designed to balance and refine your features rather than change them.

Each plan is bespoke and discussed thoroughly at consultation, with an honest assessment of what will achieve the most natural, harmonious result for you.

Meet Alana

Alana is a Registered Nurse with over 15 years of NHS experience across hospitals in London and Kent. She is currently completing Level 7+ training in Aesthetic Medicine with Harley Academy, one of the UK's leading providers of medical aesthetics education, and government JCCP approved.

She is passionate about helping clients feel confident through safe, evidence-based treatments that enhance natural beauty. Every consultation is tailored to the individual, with honesty, professionalism and client safety at the heart of everything she does.
